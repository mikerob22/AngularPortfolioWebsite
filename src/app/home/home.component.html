About Me

I recently graduated from the University of Florida with a degree in Materials Science and Engineering. I'm now an Associate Engineer at NextEra Energy - Florida Power and Light, working in Power Delivery, Transmission Engineering. My academic journey at UF provided me with a solid foundation in engineering, complemented by research experiences in areas like Magnetic Barkhausen Noise (MBN), Artificial Intelligence, and Engineering Education. In my current role, I'm contributing to NextEra Energy's mission of transforming and progressing the energy industry. It's an exciting start to my engineering career, and I'm eager to learn and grow. I'm committed to continuous professional development and aspire to explore new opportunities and make a positive impact in the industry. While working as a Transmission Engineer, I am pursuing a Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence at Florida Atlantic University to immerse myself in the fast-growing field of AI. I plan to use concepts learned to develop professionally and apply my skills in industry.
